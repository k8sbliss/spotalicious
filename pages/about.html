What is ?

Spot-a-licious is for anyone who loves and supports Food trucks!

It is a web app that allows users to track where their favorite Food Trucks are. It also allows users to share to fellow Food truck lovers whenever they spotted a Food Truck that is not on our weekly schedule.

Behind the app....

Spot-a-licious was created as the capstone project of this girl named Kate for her Front-end Web Development class at this amazing Dev Bootcamp called DevPoint Labs.

A Food Truck, which is the generic term used in this app, refers to all mobile food vendors whether its a truck, cart, or trailer. And since Kate lives in Utah, this app is for all Food Trucks in Utah.

Please be advised that since this project was done in about 30 hours throughout 9 days which includes idea brainstorming, coffee, planning, coffee, chocolates, studying/research, coding, chocolates, coffee, design, and more studying and research, coffee, and more coding, and did I say more coffee and chocolates? - that this app is still in the early stages of its life. There is definitely a plan to make this a real fully functional app with more features, so watch out or it!
